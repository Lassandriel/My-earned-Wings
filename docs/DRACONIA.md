# 🌍 World Guide: Draconia

## 🌌 The World Order

Draconia is a high fantasy setting where dragons are not mere animals or monsters, but the ruling and civilized population.

- **The Planet:** Draconia consists of giant, floating islands. Beneath these islands boils an endless sea of lava.
- **Origins:** Originally, the planet was whole, but an unknown event caused it to shatter. Most smaller islands are held together by magic or massive chains.
- **Climate:** The water from the ancient oceans is now in the atmosphere. This causes it to rain almost daily, making the islands extremely fertile with lush vegetation. Exceptions are steppes and islands without soil.
- **Government:** The world is ruled by a dual monarchy, as the planet is too large for a single ruler.

---

## 🗺️ Important Locations

- **Luxana (The Capital):** Located at the foot of an inactive volcano. The city benefits from a mild climate and sits directly between a rainforest and a savanna. Dragons from all over the empire live here.
- **The Caldera & The Sun Palace:** A gigantic volcanic crater that forms the majestic heart of the empire. Contrary to expectations, the Caldera is lushly overgrown and offers a peaceful microclimate. Here thrones the Sun Palace of the imperial family.
- **The Crystal Realm (North Portal):** A world made entirely of glowing crystals and gemstones.
- **The Emerald Jungle:** The "Green Lung" of Draconia. The plants here are so gigantic that a fully grown dragon can find shelter from the rain under a single leaf.
- **The Tree of Life:** An ancient tree whose roots reach deep into the core of the world, supplying all of Draconia with life energy.
- **The Sun Plateau:** A massive mountain above the clouds where the sun never sets. The Altar of the First Light, used for grand ceremonies, is located here.
- **The Firefly Grotto:** A sacred, underground cave system illuminated by trillions of magical beetles.

## 🧬 Biology: The Dragon Shifters

The inhabitants of Draconia call themselves Dragon Shifters. They possess a duality of being and can easily switch between two forms.

- **Dragon Form:** The true form, which varies massively depending on species and age.
- **Humanoid Form:** A human-like shape with pointed ears and scales on parts of the arms, legs, and torso.
- **Wings:** Shifters can unfold their dragon wings at any time, even in humanoid form. However, since they take up a lot of space, it is considered impolite to keep them open in everyday life without a specific reason.

**The Magic System:** Dragon magic is bound to elements, such as light, fire, earth, wind, or tides and dreams.

## 🐉 Dragon Species

Draconia is home to many completely different dragon species.

### ☀️ Light Dragons (The Rulers)

Often found in high-altitude islands and the Sun Palace. Their magic is focused on light, heat, and leadership.

- **Appearance:** Golden, white, or shimmering scales.
- **Specialty:** Solar magic and aura manipulation.

### 💎 Crystal Dragons

Native to the North Portal. Their scales are as hard as gemstones and can reflect magical attacks.

- **Appearance:** Translucent or crystalline scales in various colors.
- **Specialty:** Structural magic and energy reflection.

### 🌿 Nature Dragons

The guardians of the Emerald Jungle and the Tree of Life. They are deeply connected to the planet's vitality.

- **Appearance:** Green, brown, or floral patterns; often have vine-like appendages.
- **Specialty:** Growth acceleration and healing.

## 📜 Lore & History

### The Great Shattering

Thousands of years ago, the planet was a solid sphere. The cause of the shattering is lost to time, but some legends speak of a failed experiment to touch the core of the universe.

### The Accord of Luxana

The treaty that established the dual monarchy and ensured peace between the various dragon species. It is signed in the heart of the capital every hundred years to renew the bond.
