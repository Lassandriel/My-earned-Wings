# 🌍 World Guide: Draconia

## 🌌 The World Order
Draconia is a high fantasy setting where dragons are not mere animals or monsters, but the ruling and civilized population. 
* **The Planet:** Draconia consists of giant, floating islands. Beneath these islands boils an endless sea of lava. 
* **Origins:** Originally, the planet was whole, but an unknown event caused it to shatter. Most smaller islands are held together by magic or massive chains.
* **Climate:** The water from the ancient oceans is now in the atmosphere. This causes it to rain almost daily, making the islands extremely fertile with lush vegetation. Exceptions are steppes and islands without soil.
* **Government:** The world is ruled by a dual monarchy, as the planet is too large for a single ruler.

---

## 🗺️ Important Locations
* **Luxana (The Capital):** Located at the foot of an inactive volcano. The city benefits from a mild climate and sits directly between a rainforest and a savanna. Dragons from all over the empire live here.
* **The Caldera & The Sun Palace:** A gigantic volcanic crater that forms the majestic heart of the empire. Contrary to expectations, the Caldera is lushly overgrown and offers a peaceful microclimate. Here thrones the Sun Palace of the imperial family.
* **The Crystal Realm (North Portal):** A world made entirely of glowing crystals and gemstones. 
* **The Emerald Jungle:** The "Green Lung" of Draconia. The plants here are so gigantic that a fully grown dragon can find shelter from the rain under a single leaf.
* **The Tree of Life:** An ancient tree whose roots reach deep into the core of the world, supplying all of Draconia with life energy.
* **The Sun Plateau:** A massive mountain above the clouds where the sun never sets. The Altar of the First Light, used for grand ceremonies, is located here.
* **The Firefly Grotto:** A sacred, underground cave system illuminated by trillions of magical beetles.

## 🧬 Biology: The Dragon Shifters
The inhabitants of Draconia call themselves Dragon Shifters. They possess a duality of being and can easily switch between two forms.
* **Dragon Form:** The true form, which varies massively depending on species and age.
* **Humanoid Form:** A human-like shape with pointed ears and scales on parts of the arms, legs, and torso.
* **Wings:** Shifters can unfold their dragon wings at any time, even in humanoid form. However, since they take up a lot of space, it is considered impolite to keep them open in everyday life without a specific reason.

**The Magic System:** Dragon magic is bound to elements, such as light, fire, earth, wind, or tides and dreams.

## 🐉 Dragon Species
Draconia is home to many completely different dragon species.

### 1. Western Dragons (Guardians of the Realms)
The most widespread species, originating in the Crystal Realm.
* **Characteristics:** Large leathery wings, sturdy torso, four legs, large horns, and often an armored tail.
* **Subspecies:** Sun Dragons, Earth Dragons (robust armor), Tide/Dream Dragons (shimmering scales), Wind/Air Dragons (aerodynamic), and Crystal Dragons (gemstone scales).

### 2. Lung Dragons (Sky Walkers)
* **Characteristics:** Serpentine, graceful, and completely wingless. They have four strong legs and manes. The player belongs to this rare species.
* **Locomotion:** They fly purely through magic and "swim" or hover extremely nimbly through the air.
* **Status:** Extremely rare and often associated with the imperial bloodline. A Lung that cannot transform into their dragon form is considered a great mystery or a tragic anomaly.

### 3. Wyverns (Hunters of the Skies)
* **Characteristics:** Possess only two hind legs; their front legs are fused with their wings. They are enormously fast in the air and serve as the primary messengers of the islands.
* **Subspecies:** Storm Wyverns (generate sharp gusts of wind) and Frost Wyverns (cold-resistant).

### 4. Lindworms (The Earthbound Giants)
* **Characteristics:** Massive dragons with only two front legs and powerful, albeit heavy, wings. They drag their snake-like hindquarters behind them when on the ground.
* **Abilities:** They use their wings for powerful leaps, stabilizing themselves while climbing island cliffs, or for heavy, rhythmic flight.
* **Subspecies:** Magma Lindworms (near the lava sea) and Swamp Lindworms (perfectly camouflaged in the jungle).

### 5. Amphitheres (The Feathered Serpents)
* **Characteristics:** Rare creatures without limbs, consisting only of a serpentine body and a pair of giant wings. Their scales transition into magnificent, magic-storing plumage.
* **Subspecies:** Dream Amphitheres (generate illusionary mist) and Sun Amphitheres (glowing plumage).

### Special Form: Star Dragons
Mystical beings made of pure starlight that fall from the sky as shooting stars. They grow into giants within a few days and feed on magical energy (like star fruits or crystals). They cannot stay on Draconia and must eventually return to the starry sky.

## 🎪 Culture and Festivals
* **Festival of the First Scale:** An important ritual for young dragons. During the "ritual flight," they take on their dragon form publicly for the first time. The dragons fly around together and bestow magic upon the islands so they do not plummet into the sea of lava.
* **Tournament of Strength:** The greatest spectacle in Draconia. The most capable warriors from across the empire compete. The winner is allowed to face a member of the imperial family in an exhibition match.
