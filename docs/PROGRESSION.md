# PROGRESSION GUIDE - MY-EARNED-WINGS

## Chapter 1: The First Spark (Establishment)
The journey begins at the edge of the forest.
- **Initial Goal**: Build a **Campfire**.
- **First Bond**: Meet **Mina (Flower Girl)** to unlock foraging.
- **Shelter**: Construct a **Tent** to unlock the first Home base and the Town Hall.
- **Satiation**: Maintain your wellbeing. Low satiation reduces your **Yield** (efficiency), but no longer blocks actions entirely.

## Chapter 2: Village Life (Expansion)
Establishing roots in the community.
- **Official Registration**: Visit the **Town Hall** to register as a Resident. Unlocks the ability to work for Soul Shards.
- **Mentorship**: Meet **Teacher Aria**. She provides initial aid (**Crystal Apple**) and eventually offers a path to higher education.
- **Housing**: Build a **House** (requires a Deed from the Town Hall) to unlock advanced NPC interactions and the **Library**.
- **Tools**: Meet **Geron (Artisan)** to craft your first Axe and Pickaxe.
- **Supply**: Build **Wood & Stone Storages** to increase resource limits.

## Chapter 3: The Path of Knowledge (Education)
Expanding your mind and spirit.
- **The Library**: Study the lore books provided by Aria in your **Chronicle**.
- **Graduation**: Complete your studies to unlock the way to the **Vandara Academy**.
- **The Arcane Dream**: Meet **Master Aris** for advanced research.
- **The Sanctum**: Construct the **Arcane Sanctum** to unlock Magic and the **Arcane Focus**.

## Chapter 4: The Transformation (Demo Finale)
The final steps of the current journey.
- **Academy Vandara**: Choose your mentor (Solen, Bram, or Lyra) and specialize in your chosen path.
- **The Whispering Grove**: Discover the hidden path through Mina's questline.
- **The Tree of Life**: Reach the heart of the ground and face the final revelation of the demo.

---

### Progression Mechanics
- **Unlocks**: New actions appear based on **Flags** (Milestones) and **Registry Requirements**.
- **Yield Scaling**: Efficiency depends on Satiation (0.4x to 1.3x). High satiation grants a yield bonus!
- **Automation**: Once **Arcane Focus** is unlocked, actions can be automated using Magic.
- **Titles**: Earn unique **Titles** (like 'The Master' or 'The Wise') to gain permanent bonuses.
