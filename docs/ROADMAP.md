# Project Roadmap: Your Earned Wings ✦

This document serves as a guideline for future development. It distinguishes between necessary technical improvements and exciting content expansions.

## Phase 1: Polishing & Immersion (Technical Focus)

- [ ] **Audio Soundscape**: Implementation of ambient noises (wind, forest) and haptic sound effects for clicks (gathering, building).
- [x] **Visual Feedback ("Juice")**: Floating numbers, button pulses, and log-slide-in animations added.
- [x] **WebP Optimization**: Asset migration completed (91% size reduction).
- [x] **Expanded Resource System**: Redesigned gathering logic with specialization for tools.
- [x] **Tab Rework**: Complete visual and functional redesign of all content tabs (Main, Crafting, Housing, Inventory).
- [x] **UX Optimization**: Added save-toast confirmations, hard-reset, and rich inventory cards.

## Phase 2: Core Mechanics (Content Focus - Should)

- [x] **Tools & Progression Tier 1**: Introduction of Axe, Pickaxe, and Bow.
- [x] **Satiation & Cooking**: Fully functional satiation system with the Herd (Stove) and temporary buffs.
- [ ] **Interactive Diary**: A dedicated section for lore entries, character sketches, and progress notes.
- [ ] **Weather & Time**: Dynamic day-night cycle and weather effects (rain, fog).
- [ ] **Interior System**: Furnish the house with advanced furniture providing passive bonuses.

## Phase 3: World & Community (Content Focus - Could)

- [ ] **NPC Questlines**: Deeper storylines for the Sage, the Flower Girl, and the Blacksmith.
- [ ] **Seasons**: Visual and mechanical changes to the world throughout the in-game year.
- [ ] **Village Reputation**: A system determining how the winged beings react to your progress.

## Phase 4: The Vision (Endgame)

- [ ] **Wing Metamorphosis**: An epic goal at the end of the path – crafting your own "Wings" through magic and deep knowledge.

---

_For every step: Quality over quantity. The atmosphere ("Somber Cozy") always comes first._ ✦
